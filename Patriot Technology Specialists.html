Our history

The idea was conceived in 1998 on Camp Pendleton, Oceanside, California, when the founder, Mr. William Townes continuously struggled and crashed a home PC in the infancy of surfing the World Wide Web. Coincidentally, he met a genius US Marine Corp computer specialist who taught him to solve the problem swiftly and single-handedly. Patriot Computer, the name at that time, was born on the very day with a sparkle and two ambitious minds.

Since 1998 we have been satisfying customer needs and advancing customer profits in the fields of computer hardware, software, networking, mobile App, smart device, embedded system, IT consulting,and big data analytics.
